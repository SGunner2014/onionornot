About

Some weird project we worked on. We thought this might be a fun webgame to play for a few minutes, and as it didn't exist, spent more than a few minutes making it. Enjoy!

So what is the Onion? Some kind of vegtable? No, not really. A satirical news site. The point of the game is to sort which articles come from it, adn which are ridiculous ones from a 'normal' news organisation.
